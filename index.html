As the sole recipient from the 11th grade, I was honored with the Math Department Award at Sierra Canyon School, recognizing my consistent performance, problem-solving excellence, and leadership in mathematical communities.

Women for Math

As the founder of the Women in Math Club at both my former high school in China and later at Sierra Canyon High School in the U.S., I continuously built a bridge across cultures through mathematics. In China, I created a safe and encouraging space for girls to explore challenging problems and exchange ideas. Bringing that same spirit to Sierra Canyon, I continued leading group sessions, sharing insights, and empowering others—proving that passion for math transcends borders and backgrounds.

Chinese Drama Performance

As the producer of A Fantastic Journey of Dong Yong, I reimagined the traditional folktale of Dong Yong and the Seventh Fairy through an immersive theater experience rooted in Nanjing's intangible cultural heritage. By integrating local opera elements with interactive storytelling, I transformed a centuries-old love story into a multi-sensory journey for modern audiences. From scripting and casting to set design and audience flow, I led the entire production process to ensure both cultural authenticity and emotional resonance. The project not only revitalized traditional narratives but also introduced younger generations to the richness of regional artistry.
